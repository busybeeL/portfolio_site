  • 2023 - Present
    Savage Sisters

    Savage Sisters is my podcast where I interview Korean women working in the tech industry. It began from my curiosity about how other Korean women in tech work, grow, and live as professionals. So far, I’ve produced ten episodes—with many more to come. You can find it on any podcast app, though you’ll need to understand Korean to fully enjoy the conversations.

  • 2022 - Present
    WWTT

    WWTT stands for Wednesday Women Tech Talk. I started this study group right after COVID hit, as a way to share tech knowledge and stay connected with other Korean women working in the industry. We meet online every Wednesday evening, and each week one member prepares a presentation on a topic of their choice, which we discuss together during the session. The group is open to any Korean women living in the UK—no technical background required.
